How PF2e Encounter Math Works

In Pathfinder 2nd Edition, every encounter has an XP budget based on party level. Each creature you add contributes XP based on its level relative to the party — creatures close to party level award the most XP and have the highest challenge impact.

A creature that is 4 levels above the party awards 160 XP and acts as a "boss" in most encounters. A creature equal to party level awards 80 XP. Creatures 4+ levels below the party award only 10 XP and pose little challenge.

The default Moderate encounter budget is 80 XP for a 4-person party. A single party-level creature is a Moderate encounter. Add allies, terrain, or hazards to increase challenge without exceeding budget.

Common Encounter Recipes

Moderate Encounter (80 XP) — 4 Players

The Boss + Lackeys: 1×(Party+2) boss (80 XP) alone = Moderate. Add 2×(Party–2) minions (+40) = Severe.

The Horde: 8×(Party–4) creatures = 80 XP, exactly Moderate.

Balanced Squad: 2×(Party Level) = 80 XP = Moderate.

GM Tip: Never exceed Extreme (160 XP) unless your party has full resources and you intend a life-or-death encounter. A TPK can happen even at Severe.
